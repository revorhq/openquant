# Disclaimer

OpenQuant India is **infrastructure for research and education**.

- **Not investment advice.** Nothing in this project, its docs, its
  reference strategies, or its zoo is a recommendation to buy or sell
  any security.
- **Not a Registered Investment Adviser (RIA) service.** The
  maintainers are not RIAs. We don't sell signals or manage capital.
- **Past performance is not indicative of future results.** Backtests
  — even honest ones — are models, not promises.
- **Paper-first.** Live trading is gated behind explicit opt-in flags
  and an environment variable. Read the banner before you proceed.
- **You are responsible for your own orders.** If a strategy you wrote
  using this library loses money, that is not the project's fault.
  Test in paper first. Use the kill switch. Set max-loss caps.
- **Tax estimates are estimates.** The tax module clearly labels its
  output as `ESTIMATE — NOT TAX ADVICE`. Consult a qualified
  professional before filing.
- **NSE/BSE redistribution.** The data pipeline downloads from
  official exchange sources at the user's request. We do not
  redistribute exchange bulk data.
