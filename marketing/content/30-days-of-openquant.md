# 30 Days of OpenQuant — content kit (F5.4)

A daily X/LinkedIn post format and the 30-day prompt schedule.

## Format rules (apply to every day)

- **Punchline first.** Line one is the claim or the number.
- **One idea per post.** Cut the second one.
- **Specifics over adjectives.** Replace "huge" with "₹X" or "Y bps".
- **One screenshot or one number.** Never both as filler.
- **End with a question.** Discoverable engagement.
- **No marketing speak.** No "excited to share". Ever.

## Daily template

```
{One-sentence punchline with a number}

{2–4 lines of context. The "why this matters".}

{Code snippet OR screenshot OR one-row table.}

{Question that invites a reply.}

#OpenQuant #NSE #algotrading
```

## Distribution per day

1. Post on X around 09:30 IST (market open).
2. Cross-post to LinkedIn within 60 minutes (rewrite hook, longer body).
3. If applicable, drop in `#showcase` on Discord.
4. Subreddit cross-post only if it adds value to that sub (no link drops).

## Voice non-negotiables

- Zero "guaranteed returns" language.
- Never compare against another OSS project unfavorably.
- Always say `paper-first` when execution comes up.
- Always show a number that loses you alpha, not just one that gains it.
